### Dispute Update — UMOJA009 Late Fee
Secretary reviewed Safaricom records. The transaction was initiated on **Jan 5th at 23:47** but due to network congestion, it was processed at **Jan 6th 08:55**. The member's intent to pay was before the deadline.

*Resolution:* Late fee of KES 200 waived for UMOJA009. Refund to be applied as credit toward next month's contribution. Recorded in ledger.

### New Dispute — UMOJA012 (Awino Aoko)
Awino reported she paid February contribution of KES 5,000 to the Treasurer's personal M-Pesa number (+254733013013) by mistake instead of the paybill. Treasurer Kamau confirmed receipt of KES 5,000 in personal account on Feb 5th at approximately 11:30 AM.

*Issue 1:* Treasurer had not proactively notified the Chama or transferred the funds.
*Issue 2:* Member was charged a late fee of KES 200 for the "missing" February payment.

*Chairperson's ruling (provisional):* Treasurer to transfer KES 5,000 to chama paybill within 48 hours. Late fee of KES 200 waived. Treasurer Kamau issued a formal apology. Mediation panel to be convened if transfer not made by Mar 12th.

*Update (Mar 13):* Transfer confirmed RKT86099001. Case closed.

### Investment Discussion
Treasurer Rotich proposed investing KES 200,000 of the pool in a Treasury Bill (364-day, approx 16% p.a.). To be voted at April meeting after members review the proposal.

### Dispute Resolution — UMOJA017 Late Fee
Mediation Panel (Otieno, Chebet, Kipchoge) report:
- Reviewed Safaricom complaint ref SF-CMP-2024-04-092847.
- Safaricom confirmed the paybill service was intermittently unavailable Apr 5th, 2:00 PM – 3:30 PM due to scheduled maintenance (Safaricom Maintenance Notice MNT-2024-0405).
- Member's failed transaction screenshot timestamp (2:14 PM) falls within confirmed maintenance window.
- Member subsequently paid on Apr 22nd when the issue was resolved.

*Panel decision:* Late fee of KES 500 waived. Member Ombogo is not at fault. Chama bylaws Article 5.2 presupposes payment system availability; force majeure applies. Treasurer to reverse late fee and update ledger.

*UMOJA017 accepted the ruling with thanks.*
